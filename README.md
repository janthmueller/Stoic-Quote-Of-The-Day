<p align="center">
    <em><!--START_SECTION:quote-text-->
The whole Universe is change, and life itself is but what you deem it.
<!--END_SECTION:quote-text--></em><br>
    <strong>— <!--START_SECTION:quote-author-->
Marcus Aurelius
<!--END_SECTION:quote-author--></strong>
</p>

<div align="center" style="max-width:600px;margin:0 auto;">
<!--START_SECTION:quote-interpretation-->
Change is the one constant within the vastness of the universe; everything is in perpetual flux. Recognizing that life is shaped by our perceptions and interpretations allows us to embrace this reality without trepidation. In our daily lives, we often face unexpected changes, such as a new job, moving to a different city, or shifts in personal relationships. While our initial reaction might veer towards stress or resistance, understanding that we have the power to define our experience through our mindset creates a pathway to resilience. Instead of seeing change as a source of fear, we can reframe it as an opportunity for growth and renewal. By focusing on our judgments and how we choose to react to life’s inevitable transformations, we exercise control over our inner world. This perspective doesn’t merely ease our anxieties about change but actively empowers us to navigate life with greater equanimity and purpose, fostering a sense of inner peace regardless of external circumstances.
<!--END_SECTION:quote-interpretation-->
